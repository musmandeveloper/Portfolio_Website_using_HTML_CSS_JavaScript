


                           -------------------------------------------------------------------------
                           |   ### **Detailed Color, Style, and Design Scheme for Your Website**   |
                           -------------------------------------------------------------------------




----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This scheme focuses on various elements such as **primary/secondary colors**, **hover effects**, **sections**, and **link styling** 
to ensure a cohesive and polished design.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------




### 1. **Primary Color**
- **Hex Code**: `#1A76D1`
- **Use**: The primary color is used for key interactive elements such as buttons, links (on hover), and active states. It creates a 
    visual hierarchy and encourages users to engage.
  
---

### 2. **Secondary Color**
- **Hex Code**: `#007BFF`
- **Use**: This secondary color complements the primary color and can be used for secondary call-to-action buttons, subtle accents, 
    or as a color for non-primary elements (sub-links, secondary buttons).

---

### 3. **Text Color**
- **Hex Code**: `#2C2D3F`
- **Use**: General text, non-hovered links, and descriptions. This dark color ensures high readability across light backgrounds.

---

### 4. **Background Color**
- **Hex Code**: `#ffffff` (White)
- **Use**: The background color is clean and neutral, ensuring that the content is easy to read and that primary/secondary colors 
    stand out.

---

### 5. **Topbar Links Color**
- **Normal Color**: `#2C2D3F`
- **Hover Color**: `#1A76D1`
- **Use**: For the links displayed in the **top navigation bar**. The hover color switches to the primary color to show interactivity.

---

### 6. **Main Menu Item Colors**
- **Normal Color**: `#2C2D3F`
- **Hover Color**: `#1A76D1`
- **Use**: For the main navigation bar, providing consistency with topbar links. The primary color on hover highlights user interaction.

---

### 7. **Sub-Menu Item Colors**
- **Normal Color**: `#2C2D3F`
- **Hover Color**: `#007BFF` (Secondary color)
- **Use**: For dropdown or sub-items under the main navigation links. A subtler hover color differentiates them from primary menu items 
    while keeping the design consistent.

---

### 8. **Section Headings (H1-H6) Colors**
- **Color**: `#000000`
- **Use**: Section headings should be bold and clearly visible. Black is used to give a strong emphasis and create visual separation 
    between sections.

---

### 9. **Project/Posts Lists Colors**
- **Title**: `#1A76D1` (Primary Color)
- **Date/Time/Category**: `#888888` (Gray for metadata)
- **Description**: `#2C2D3F` (Regular text color)
- **Hover Effect**: On hovering over the **title**, it changes to a darker tone of the primary color for better contrast.

---

### 10. **Single Page (Projects/Blogs/Posts)**
- **Title**: `#1A76D1` (Primary Color)
- **Content Text**: `#2C2D3F`
- **Background Color**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Hover Color for Internal Links**: `#007BFF` (Secondary color)
- **Hover Color for External Links**: `#FF0000` (To highlight external navigation)

---

### 11. **"You May Like" (Suggested Posts/Related Posts)**
- **Link Color**: `#1A76D1` (Primary color for post links)
- **Hover Color**: `#007BFF`
- **Description**: `#2C2D3F` (Regular text)

---

### 12. **Sidebars and Widgets (Sidebars Sections, Links)**
- **Sidebar Background**: `#F8F8F8` (Light grey)
- **Sidebar Heading**: `#1A76D1` (Primary color to match other section titles)
- **Sidebar Links**: `#2C2D3F`
- **Sidebar Link Hover Color**: `#007BFF` (Secondary color for consistency)

---

### 13. **Buttons Color Scheme**
- **Primary Button**:
  - Background: `#1A76D1`
  - Text: `#FFFFFF`
  - Hover Background: `#144a7c` (Darker tone of primary color)
  
- **Secondary Button**:
  - Background: `#007BFF`
  - Text: `#FFFFFF`
  - Hover Background: `#0056b3` (Darker tone of secondary color)

---

### 14. **Comments Section Color Scheme**
- **User Name**: `#1A76D1` (Primary color for commenter's name)
- **Comment Date**: `#888888` (Grey for date/timestamps)
- **Comment Text**: `#2C2D3F`
- **Reply/Interaction Links**: `#007BFF` (Secondary color)
- **Reply Link Hover**: `#1A76D1` (Primary color)

---

### 15. **Footer Color Scheme**
- **Footer Background**: `#222222` (Dark grey)
- **Footer Column Headings**: `#FFFFFF` (White for readability)
- **Footer Links**: 
  - Normal: `#BBBBBB` (Light grey for subtlety)
  - Hover: `#1A76D1` (Primary color)
- **Footer Text**: `#BBBBBB` (Light grey for general text)

---

### **Overview of the Design Scheme**

1. **Primary Color**: `#1A76D1`  
   - Used for: Buttons, hovered links, and key visual elements.
   
2. **Secondary Color**: `#007BFF`  
   - Used for: Sub-menu links, secondary buttons, and some hover effects.

3. **Text Color**: `#2C2D3F`  
   - Used for: General text and descriptions.

4. **Background Color**: `#FFFFFF`  
   - Used for: Body background and content sections.

5. **Topbar Links**:  
   - Normal: `#2C2D3F`
   - Hover: `#1A76D1` (Primary color)

6. **Main Menu Links**:  
   - Normal: `#2C2D3F`
   - Hover: `#1A76D1`

7. **Sub-Menu Links**:  
   - Normal: `#2C2D3F`
   - Hover: `#007BFF`

8. **Headings**: `#000000`  
   - Used for: Section titles and headings.

9. **Posts/Projects Titles**: `#1A76D1`  
   - Metadata: `#888888`
   - Description: `#2C2D3F`

10. **Single Page Titles**: `#1A76D1`  
    - Hover on Links: `#007BFF` (Internal) or `#FF0000` (External)

11. **Sidebar**:  
    - Background: `#F8F8F8`
    - Headings: `#1A76D1`
    - Links: `#2C2D3F`
    - Hover Links: `#007BFF`

12. **Buttons**:  
    - Primary: `#1A76D1`, hover: `#144a7c`
    - Secondary: `#007BFF`, hover: `#0056b3`

13. **Footer**:  
    - Background: `#222222`
    - Headings: `#FFFFFF`
    - Links: `#BBBBBB`, hover: `#1A76D1`



---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This detailed breakdown ensures a consistent, clean, and professional design across all areas of your website.
